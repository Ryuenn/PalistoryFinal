2025 Health Conference + Gala Fundraiser

Saturday – Sunday, October 11–12, 2025
Mohammed Schools of Atlanta, 735 Fayetteville Rd SE, Atlanta, GA 30316

Sisters United in Human Service, Inc., in collaboration with the Interfaith Health and Human Service Conference, hosted a two-day event blending educational sessions with celebration to strengthen community health and services.

Empowering Communities Through Health & Service

The gathering brought together healthcare professionals, faith leaders, and community advocates for discussions on critical health and human service matters — featuring panels, workshops, and networking aimed at advancing resilience and access to care.

Gala Fundraiser: An Evening of Inspiration

The weekend concluded with a fundraising celebration honoring service and supporting community programs through fellowship and charitable giving.

"Palistory shared the powerful stories of Palestine through imagery and narrative — bringing themes of resilience, justice, and compassion to the conference programming."

Thank You

We're grateful to everyone who learned, connected, and gave generously — proving once again that community care and cultural storytelling go hand in hand.
