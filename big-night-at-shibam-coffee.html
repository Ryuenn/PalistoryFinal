Big Night at Shibam Coffee

May 14, 2025
Shibam Coffee

We teamed up with Shibam to premiere an exclusive sneak peek of the powerful documentary we’ve been working on — a raw, moving look into Palestinian lives from the Nakba until now.

"Because facts inform…
but stories transform."

At Palistory, we’re not just preserving history — we’re amplifying voices that the world tried to silence. Every testimony, every image, every moment matters.

A Special Thank You

Thank you to @shibamcoffee for opening your beautiful space and hosting a night of purpose, passion, and Palestinian resilience.
