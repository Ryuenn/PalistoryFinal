18th Annual Atlanta Arab Festival

Saturday – Sunday, October 4–5, 2025
Alif Institute, 3288 Marjan Drive, Atlanta, GA 30340

Hosted by the Alif Institute, the Atlanta Arab Festival (AAF) is an annual event celebrating Arab and Arab-American heritage through community building and cultural exchange — a weekend filled with Arabic flavors, sights, and sounds.

The festival featured live stage performances, a food court showcasing Arab cuisine, a souq (marketplace) for shopping, plus exhibits, workshops, and games — all in support of the Institute's mission to foster understanding, appreciation, and preservation of Arab cultures.

"A weekend filled with Arabic flavors, sights, and sounds — celebrating Arab and Arab-American heritage."

Thank You

We were honored to take part in this celebration of culture and community — sharing Palestinian stories alongside the rich tapestry of Arab heritage on display.
