PCRF: The Children of Gaza

July 26, 2025
Charity & Fundraiser

On July 26, 2025, Palistory hosted a special event in support of the Palestine Children’s Relief Fund (PCRF), dedicated to providing urgent medical care, humanitarian aid, and hope to the children of Gaza.

A Night of Hope and Healing

As the situation in Gaza continues to demand our immediate attention and unwavering support, this gathering served as a powerful beacon of solidarity. Every ticket, donation, and shared story directly contributed to PCRF's life-saving missions on the ground, delivering critical relief to those who need it most.

"Compassion is not just a feeling; it is an action. Together, we can rebuild hope and provide healing for the children of Gaza."

Standing Together

Through powerful testimonies, community reflections, and a shared commitment to justice, we aimed to amplify the voices of the most vulnerable. It was an honor to see our community join hands to fund critical medical interventions and stand firm in the belief that every child deserves a future free from suffering.

Continuing the Impact

Our deepest gratitude to everyone who attended, donated, and raised their voices. While this event has concluded, our work alongside PCRF continues. Together, we are writing a story of resilience and enduring care.
