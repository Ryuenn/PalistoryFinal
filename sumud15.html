Why it matters

Fifteen days of chosen discipline. For the fifteenth of May, nineteen forty-eight.

This challenge does not compare chosen difficulty to a lifetime of forced exile. It claims remembrance. Every day we walked, every page we read, and every contribution we secured pointed directly back to stories the world has tried to forget.

The daily standard

The 3 daily rules we kept

The rigorous framework our participants successfully upheld for fifteen consecutive days.

Walk 30 Minutes Every Day

One unbroken walk every day for 15 days. Outside is strongly preferred, but a treadmill is acceptable when needed.

Read 10 Pages + Share One Insight

Read nonfiction and post one daily insight to your Instagram story. Tag @Palistory.usa and use #Sumud15.

Clean Hydration / No Added Sugar

Water first. Unsweetened coffee and tea are allowed. No soda. No added sugar. Honey is allowed up to one tablespoon per day.

Frequently asked questions

Can I run the challenge on my own schedule?

While the official group cohort is over, you are welcome to adopt the three guidelines (walk, read, hydration) on your own terms to support individual discipline.

How often does Palistory host Sumud 15?

Sumud 15 is scheduled as an annual landmark challenge. Join our waitlist to stay informed about dates and details for future cohorts.

Can I still get a keffiyeh or a stole?

These specialized sets were exclusive to active challenge registrants and sponsors. However, you can keep an eye on Palistory's general storefront for future releases.

Are general donations still open?

Yes. Although the specific event is closed, you can support Palistory's archival and educational projects directly on our homepage at any time.
