Palistory at GAZA 5K – Atlanta

February 21 @ 8:00 am - 5:00 pm
Atlanta, GA

Palistory proudly participated in the GAZA 5K in Atlanta, organized by UNRWA. This impactful annual event brings together communities across the United States in solidarity with Palestine refugee children.

"The GAZA 5K raises critical funds to support mental health counseling and trauma care services for refugee children affected by ongoing hardship."

The atmosphere was filled with unity, compassion, and purpose as participants walked and ran to stand for healing and hope.

Palistory was honored to show our support and stand alongside advocates, families, and community leaders who believe in access to mental health resources for every child.

Meaningful Cause

We are grateful to everyone who participated and contributed to this meaningful cause. Together, we continue to amplify awareness and support initiatives that foster resilience and care.
